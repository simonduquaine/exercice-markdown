### **1.2 Headers**

Headers go from 1 (the largest) to 6 (the smallest) and are written like this :
  
 * `# H1`<br/> 
 * `## H2`<br/>
 * `### H3` <br/>
 * `#### H4`<br/>
 * `##### H5`<br/>
 * `###### H6`<br/>

#### Dispayed :

 # H1
 ## H2
 ### H3
 #### H4
 ##### H5
 ###### H6

 *But there is another way to write `# H1` and `## H2` if needed :*

`H1`</br>
`===========`

`H2`</br>
`-----------`

#### Dispayed :

H1
===

H2
---
